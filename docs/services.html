Services

We design custom drilling and blasting for lakes and utilities developing commercial, government, industrial, mining, and residential projects.

Our services include…

  • Exploratory drilling services to a max of 25 feet.
  • Custom water drilling services that can reach depths of 85 feet from working surface.
  • Unmanned Aerial Vehicle Operations (UAV) Services.
  • Blasting and Drilling Advising.

Licenses & Certifications

  • ATF-E Licenses and possessors’ approval.
  • State of Florida User and Blaster licenses.
  • State Fire Marshall Licenses.
  • Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) certification.
  • United States Department of Transportation (USDOT) regulations, endorsements, and permits.
  • Hazardous Materials Training.
  • MSHA Blue card approved instructors.
  • Red Cross CPR First Aid approved instructors.
  • FAA Part 107 trained Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) operator.
  • Commercial Vehicle Licenses.
  • International Maritime Dangerous Goods certified shipper (for shipping overseas hazmat).
  • Occupational Safety Hazard Association (OSHA) Fall Protection competent person.
  • United States Coast Guard (USCG) licensed master captain, up to 100 gross tons.
  • An educational partnership with Medical, Fire, & Law Enforcement Services. As well as working with homes owners and communities.
